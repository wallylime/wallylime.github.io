About Meridian, Idaho

Population & Economy

Located in the Boise, Idaho metro, Meridian is one of the fastest-growing cities in the United States and has a strong economy.[1] In 2010, 75,092 people lived in Meridian. According to the latest estimate in 2019, that number had grown to 114,161 people. The median household income for Meridian between 2015 and 2019 was $71,389.[2] The May 2021 unemployment rate for the Boise metro was 2.9%[3] compared to the national unemployment rate of 5.8%.[4]
