Join the Chamber of Commerce

Benefits of Joining

The Chamber of Commerce helps businesses in Meridian to thrive through networking, mentorship, advertising, and community outreach. Join today to help your business succeed!

Non-Profit Membership - $100

  • your business will be listed in our directory
  • you can attend Chamber of Commerce meetings and vote on Chamber of Commerce leadership

Bronze Membership - $200

  • all the benefits of the Non-Profit Membership
  • free admission to Chamber-sponsored business seminars

Silver Membership - $400

  • all the benefits of the Non-Profit and Bronze Membership Levels
  • your business will be featured on the Chamber's home page for two months
  • one social media post from the Chamber of Commerce highlighting your business
  • 50% discount on admission to weekly networking luncheon

Gold Membership - $1000

  • all the benefits of the Non-Profit, Bronze, and Silver Membership Levels
  • your business will be featured on the Chamber's home page for the duration of your membership
  • free admission to weekly networking luncheon
