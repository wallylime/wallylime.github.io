Serious Accident on Government Dam Road

A teenage driver flipped her SUV while driving around a curve on Government Dam Road. She wasn't wearing a seatbelt and was thrown from the vehicle. Because she sustained serious injuries, the deputy on the scene called life-flight which transported the teenager to a hospital in Pocatello for medical care.
