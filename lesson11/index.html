Extreme Heat Continues in Southeast Idaho

Forget the barbeque and pull out the popsicles and ice cream this 4th of July! The current heat wave is expected to continue through the July 4th weekend. Record-setting temperatures are possible and residents are advised to use caution in any outdoor activities. Furthermore, residents are asked to reduce energy consumption between 4-9pm in order to avoid power outages. Read more about it in this article: "Scorching Heat Wave to Continue in East Idaho, Could Last Through July 4 Weekend" by Shelbie Harris.
