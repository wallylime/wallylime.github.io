Bear Lake Trout Controversy

Idaho Fish & Game is concerned that salmon in Stanley Lake may be at risk due to large trout populations. In an effort to ameliorate this risk, Idaho Fish & Game has come up with a plan to remove trout from Stanley Lake and replace the trout with sterile trout netted out of Bear Lake. There are many people opposed to this plan. To read more about it, go to this article by Rod Boam: "Some Bear Lakers Strongly Oppose Idaho's Plan to Net Lake Trout."
