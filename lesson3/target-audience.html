Site Visitor Demographics

Gender: Male and Female
Age: Generally mid-20's and older
Location: Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven, Idaho
Income: Varies
Site Access: 60% through mobile device, 40% through desktop
Motivation: Desire to know the weather, local news, and upcoming events.

Personas
