Recipe Tutorial

All of my recipes are written in a table format. The first column of the table is for ingredients and the second column of the table is for the directions that go with those particular ingredients. You can think of every new row of the table as a new step. I won’t list any ingredients on a row unless they are used in the corresponding directions’ column of that row. Just follow the table from left to right, top to bottom.
Here’s an example...
In the chocolate chip cookie recipe below, you would first take 12 T. of melted butter, 1 C. of light brown sugar, and 1/2 C. of sugar and cream them together. Then you would go to the next row and take 1 large egg, 1 large egg yolk, and 2 t. vanilla and mix those in with your ingredients from the first step. Then you would take the ingredients from the next row down and add those in. Finally, you would bake the cookies at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes. See how easy it is!?!

Chocolate Chip Cookies
Ingredients Directions
12 T. melted butter
1 C. light brown sugar
1/2 C. sugar		 Cream together
1 large egg
1 large egg yolk
2 t. vanilla		 Mix in
2 1/8 C. flour
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t. baking soda
2 C. chocolate chips
 Add and mix together
Bake at 350 for 10-12 min.
